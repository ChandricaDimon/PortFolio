Hello!

My name is Chandrica Dimon, but you can call me Rica. For as long as I can remember art has been my passion. As a young girl I would doodle on just about everything, from paper to my bedroom walls. Although my mom was not always pleased to see me painting on my nice clean walls, the final work of art was enough for her to forget about. My free time would regularly consist of designing my own patterns to taking my patterns and designs to incorporate into a bigger picture. My passion played a great role in my life and helped guide me to my career path.
